4. 7.5% Government School Reservation (Special Quota)

As per Tamil Nadu Act No.34 of 2020:

✓ 7.5% of Government seats are reserved for students who studied Class 6 to 12 in Government Schools.

Eligible Schools

  • Government Schools
  • Corporation Schools
  • Municipal Schools
  • Adi Dravidar Schools
  • Tribal Welfare Schools
  • Kallar Schools
  • Forest Dept Schools

Important Rules

  • Eligible students can compete in general quota also.
  • Reservation follows Tamil Nadu rules.
  • If seats remain vacant → given to private school students.
  • Students under this quota cannot discontinue.
  • If discontinued → must pay: Discontinuation fee, Tuition fee, Hostel fee, Mess fee, University fee, Other expenses
